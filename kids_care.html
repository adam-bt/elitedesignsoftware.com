Kids Care

Kids Care automates and maximizes child safety when transferring children between child care facilities and parents.

Kids Care provides an extremely safe and efficient child transfer process allowing large volumes of children to be processed very quickly making it ideal for child care centres, vacation care, churches and youth groups.

Detailed parent and child details can be stored providing you up-to-date information as well as fast access to allergy information and emergency contact details.

Powerful reporting tools assist you to identify attendance trends, monitor absent children, generate mailing lists and highlight children's birthdays. Reports can be completely customized for your organisation.
