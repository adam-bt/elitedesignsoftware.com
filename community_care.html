Community Care is a comprehensive client management system designed for charities to:
  • Maintain client data
  • Detail client interactions
  • Automate referrals
  • Track resource distributions
  • Identify trends

Community Care allows you to easily enter your client's information and track interactions with them while also cross-matching their previous visits.

Referrals are automatically generated, maximizing time spent with the client rather than manually preparing paperwork.

Powerful reports monitor trends, client needs, demographics, referrals, track resource distributions, and provide detailed analysis of all client interactions. The reports can be customized for your organisation.
